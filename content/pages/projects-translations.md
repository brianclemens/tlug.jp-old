1. Overview
The Translations project will be a point to refer for all the peoples that want to translate some pages of this wiki in their language.

We will use also the category [Translations](//category/translations/)
1. Language SubProjects
Every language will have a subproject page on Project:Translations:Language (es: [Projects:Translations:Italian](/projects/translations/italian/)).

The page of the subproject will be written in the language of the subproject.
