1. << Presentation (1) >>
**"Reading Felica cards from Linux/BSD"** by *[Lars Kotthoff](/lars kotthoff/)*

This month, [Lars Kotthoff](/lars kotthoff/) will be explaining how to use libpasori[[1](http://libpasori.sourceforge.jp/)] to
read Felica cards from Linux.  He will also demonstrate a Ruby
application he wrote to read Suica/Pasmo cards and display information
about the last 20 transactions[[2](http://www.larsko.org/PasoriRuby)].

*[1] http://libpasori.sourceforge.jp/
*[2] http://www.larsko.org/PasoriRuby

1. << Presentation (2) >>

**"Happy Tracing with DTrace"** by *[Hisayoshi Kato](/hisayoshi kato/)*

Our second speaker, [Hisayoshi Kato](/hisayoshi kato/) will give an overview of DTrace[[1](http://opensolaris.org/os/community/dtrace/)][[2](http://www.sun.com/bigadmin/content/dtrace/)][[3](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DTrace)].  He
will demonstrate some of the many applications of DTrace probes,
including Java Tracing, Python Tracing and OS Tracing.

*[1] http://opensolaris.org/os/community/dtrace/
*[2] http://www.sun.com/bigadmin/content/dtrace/
*[3] http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DTrace
