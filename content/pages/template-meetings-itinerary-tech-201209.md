1. Itinerary

1. Presentations

1. Enterprise Provisioning and Configuration Management of RHEL systems (by [Ogilvie](Glen)(/glen ogilvie/))

Introduction to the goals and aims of provisioning systems and managing the configuration of many RHEL servers accross multiple enviroments from an enterprise point of view.  Following this, a brief overview of how [Red Hat Network Satellite](http://www.redhat.com/products/enterprise-linux/rhn-satellite/) and [Puppet](http://puppetlabs.com/puppet/what-is-puppet/) can be combined to achieve these goals.  Then taking a deep technical dive into the steps a server might go through to go from bare metal to having a web application up and running, using these tools.

1. OSCON Report (by [Ohta](Masafumi)(/masafumi ohta/))

Ohta-san attended the O'Reilly Open Source Conference ([OSCON 2012](http://www.oscon.com/oscon2012)), that took place in Portland, OR, last July. He also attended the Community Leadership Summit ([CLS12](http://www.communityleadershipsummit.com/about/)), the event started by Jono Bacon, the community manager for the global Ubuntu community and author of The Art of Community.

In his talk, Ohta-san will summarize the sessions he attended in these two events, talking about how people are contributing to the Open Source community, latest activities, and more.
