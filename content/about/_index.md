# About the Tokyo Linux Users Group

The Tokyo Linux Users Group (TLUG) is a group of Linux users and enthusiasts who meet regularly in Tokyo, Japan.

## History

TLUG was founded in September 1994, making it one of the oldest Linux user groups in Asia. Since then, we have:

- Held hundreds of technical meetings featuring presentations from community members and industry experts
- Provided a forum for Linux users in Tokyo to share knowledge and experiences
- Contributed to the growth of the open source community in Japan
- Built a repository of helpful guides and documentation

## Organization

TLUG is an informal, volunteer-run organization. We have no official membership fees or formal structure. The group is sustained by the enthusiasm and contributions of its members.

## Code of Conduct

TLUG is committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all members. We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and considerate of others
- Refrain from discriminatory or harassing behavior
- Help create a positive community atmosphere
- Welcome newcomers and help them feel included
