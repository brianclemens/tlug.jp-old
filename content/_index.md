# Welcome to TLUG

The **Tokyo Linux Users Group** (TLUG) is a group of Linux users and enthusiasts who meet regularly in Tokyo, Japan. We've been fostering the Linux and open source community in Tokyo since 1994.

## What We Do

- **Technical Meetings**: Monthly presentations and discussions about Linux, open source, and related technologies
- **Social Events (Nomikai)**: Regular after-meeting gatherings and social events
- **Mailing List**: Active community discussions and support
- **Wiki & Resources**: Guides, tips, and documentation

## Upcoming Meetings

Check our [Meetings](/meetings/) page for the latest schedule.

## Join Us

Everyone is welcome at TLUG meetings! You don't need to be a Linux expert. Whether you're just curious about Linux, a seasoned sysadmin, or anywhere in between, you'll find friendly people and interesting discussions.

### How to Join

- **Mailing List**: Subscribe to our [mailing list](https://lists.tlug.jp/) for announcements and discussions
- **Matrix Room**: Join us on [Matrix](https://matrix.to/#/#tlug.jp:matrix.org) for real-time chat
- **Just Show Up**: Come to any of our meetings - no registration required!

## History

TLUG was founded in 1994, making it one of the oldest Linux user groups in Asia. Over the years, we've hosted hundreds of technical presentations, social events, and contributed to the growth of the open source community in Japan.
