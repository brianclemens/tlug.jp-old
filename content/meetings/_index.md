# TLUG Meetings

TLUG holds regular meetings in Tokyo:

- **Technical Meetings**: Usually on the second Saturday of each month (13:00-15:00)
- **Nomikai (Social Events)**: Usually on the second Friday of each month (19:30)

## Upcoming Meetings

Browse the meeting archives below or check our [mailing list](https://lists.tlug.jp/) for the latest announcements.
